# Golden Visa Application Workflow

## Overview
This workflow outlines the complete process for obtaining a Greek Golden Visa through property investment. The process takes approximately 3-12 months from start to finish.

## Key Requirements
- Minimum property investment: €250,000
- Total estimated costs including fees: ~€296,390 USD
- Valid passport and required documentation
- Power of Attorney for remote processing

## Process Steps

### Phase 1: Legal Preparation (1-7 days)

#### Step 1: Power of Attorney (POA) Preparation
**If client travels to Greece:**
- Draft POA with trusted lawyer
- Sign at Greek Notary
- **Timeline:** Completion in 1 day

**If client is remote:**
- Draft POA locally (Notary or Greek Embassy)
- Authenticate with Apostille
- Translate to English and Apostille translation
- Send original documents to Athens
- Greek translation and submission
- **Timeline:** Up to 4 days

#### Step 2: Tax ID and Bank Account
- Obtain Greek TAX ID Number (requires POA & Passport)
- Open Greek Bank Account
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days each

### Phase 2: Property Purchase (2-3 weeks)

#### Step 3: Private Purchase Agreement
- Draft and sign private agreement
- Detail purchase terms and deposit
- **Timeline:** 1-2 days

#### Step 4: Payment Schedule
- Initial Deposit: €5,000 upon reservation
- Upon Private Agreement: 10% of purchase price
- Second Payment: 10% by end of February
- Final Payment: Remaining balance at final contract

#### Step 5: Final Sale Preparation
- Legal team prepares final contract
- Client prepares Golden Visa documents
- **Timeline:** 2-3 weeks

#### Step 6: Full Payment and Final Contract
- Transfer remaining balance
- Sign final contract
- Receive essential notary documents
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days for documents

### Phase 3: Golden Visa Application (3-12 months)

#### Step 7: Document Preparation
**Main Applicant Required Documents:**
- Power of Attorney (apostilled if foreign)
- Passport (all pages scanned)
- Final Purchase Agreement
- Proof of Deposit at Land Registry
- Certificate of Registration
- Lawyer's and Notary's Certifications
- Fee payment: €2,060
- Life Insurance Policy
- Birth Certificate (with apostille, Latin characters)

**Additional Family Members:**
- Spouse: Additional €150 fee, marriage certificate
- Minor Children: €150 each, birth certificates
- Parents: €150 each, birth certificate of main investor
- Parents-in-law: €150 each, additional documentation

#### Step 8: Visa Application Submission
- Submit all documents for main investor and family
- **Timeline:** 5-7 business days

#### Step 9: Processing Steps
- Confirmation of Submission (same day)
- Proof of Application Receipt - "White Paper" (few weeks)
- Biometric Appointment (2-3 weeks after submission)
- Visa Issuance (3-12 months processing time)

#### Step 10: Final Visa Issuance
- Lawyer collects visa or client picks up personally
- Enjoy visa-free travel in Europe

## Cost Breakdown
- Property Price: €250,000 (~$270,000 USD)
- Notary Fee: 2% (~$5,400 USD)
- Lawyer Fee (Property): 2% (~$5,400 USD)
- Lawyer Fee (Golden Visa): €2,500 + €200/family member (~$2,700 USD)
- Government Golden Visa Fee: €2,016 + €166/family member (~$2,177 USD)
- Transfer Tax: 3.09% (~$8,283 USD)
- Land Registration Fee: 0.8% (~$2,160 USD)
- Life Insurance: ~$250-270 USD
- **Total Additional Costs:** ~$26,390 USD
- **Total Investment:** ~$296,390 USD

## Important Notes
- Additional costs for Golden Visa issuance: 10% of property price
- All family members must be included in initial application
- Timeline can vary based on Ministry processing
- Continuous monitoring by legal team throughout process
