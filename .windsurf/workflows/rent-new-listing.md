# Rent - New Listing Workflow

## Overview
This workflow outlines the complete process for renting out a new property listing through the agency, from initial client inquiry to successful tenant placement.

## Service Details
- **Agency Fee:** One month's rent + VAT
- **Listing Channels:** Company website, Spitogatos, Xrysi Eukeria, and other advertising platforms
- **Target:** Long-term rentals only

## Process Steps

### Phase 1: Client Onboarding

#### Step 1: Client Inquiry
- Homeowner contacts agency via:
  - Website form
  - Phone call
  - Email
  - Referral
- Collect initial property information

#### Step 2: Pre-Listing Requirements
- Send comprehensive requirements email to client
- Request property details:
  - Floor plan
  - Location specifics
  - Property photos (if available)
  - Rental price expectations
- Disclose agency fee structure (one month's rent + VAT)
- Send listing agreement for legal authorization

### Phase 2: Property Assessment & Marketing

#### Step 3: Initial Meeting & Property Visit
- Schedule property visit if interested
- Assess property condition and rental potential
- **Decision Point:** Accept or decline listing

#### Step 4: Photography & Listing Creation
- **Option A:** Sales team creates photo record
- **Option B:** Arrange professional photoshoot (paid service)
- Create compelling property listing
- Publish across all advertising channels:
  - Company website
  - Spitogatos
  - Xrysi Eukeria
  - Other relevant platforms

### Phase 3: Tenant Acquisition

#### Step 5: Receiving Tenant Inquiries
- Monitor and respond to incoming inquiries
- Pre-screen potential tenants
- Gather basic tenant information

#### Step 6: Property Viewings
- Schedule and conduct property viewings
- Agent attends all viewings
- Share listing agreement with interested tenants
- Collect tenant information:
  - Contact details
  - Employment/income verification
  - Rental history
  - References
- Obtain tenant agreement to pay agency commission

### Phase 4: Agreement & Finalization

#### Step 7: Rental Agreement Drafting
- Select qualified tenant
- Draft comprehensive rental agreement
- Include all terms and conditions
- Specify rent amount, deposit, and duration

#### Step 8: Contract Signing
- Send agreement to both parties:
  - Homeowner (landlord)
  - Tenant
- Collect signatures from both parties
- Ensure all legal requirements are met

#### Step 9: Payment Collection
- Tenant payments due upon signing:
  - Security deposit (typically 1-3 months' rent)
  - First month's rent
  - Agency fee (one month's rent + VAT)
- Process all payments securely

#### Step 10: Invoicing & Documentation
- Send invoices to:
  - Homeowner (for services rendered)
  - Tenant (for payment confirmation)
- Provide receipts and documentation
- Maintain complete transaction records

### Phase 5: Post-Rental Management

#### Step 11: Listing Management
- Mark property as "rented" on all platforms
- Alternatively, delete active listings
- Update property status across channels

#### Step 12: Handover
- Coordinate property handover to tenant
- Document property condition
- Provide keys to tenant
- Establish ongoing communication channels

## Required Documents

### From Property Owner
- Signed listing agreement
- Property ownership proof
- Floor plan
- Property photos
- Utility bills (if applicable)
- Building regulations (if applicable)

### From Tenant
- Completed application form
- ID/Passport copy
- Proof of income/employment
- Rental references
- Signed rental agreement
- Agency fee agreement

## Timeline Estimates
- **Step 1-2 (Onboarding):** 2-3 days
- **Step 3-4 (Marketing):** 3-5 days
- **Step 5-6 (Viewings):** 1-3 weeks
- **Step 7-10 (Closing):** 2-5 days
- **Total Process:** 2-4 weeks average

## Important Notes
- All tenants must agree to pay agency commission
- Agency fee is non-refundable once paid
- Property must meet minimum rental standards
- All agreements must be in writing
- Maintain professional communication throughout process
