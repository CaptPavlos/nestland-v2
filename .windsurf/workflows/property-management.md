# Property Management Workflow

## Overview
This workflow outlines the process for taking on long-term rental property management. The service is offered primarily to Golden Visa clients but is also available to external clients.

## Service Details
- **Service Type:** Long-term property management only
- **Fee Structure:** 15% of annual rents + VAT
- **Target Clients:** Golden Visa property owners (100% return rate) and external property owners

## Process Steps

### Phase 1: Initial Contact & Assessment

#### Step 1: Client Request
- Client submits property management request
- **Contact Methods:** 
  - Direct to Johan (existing GV clients)
  - Email/phone to team/office
  - Website inquiry
- **Required Information:** Property address, photos if available

#### Step 2: Property Evaluation
- Schedule and conduct property visit
- Assess property condition and suitability
- Evaluate rental potential
- **Decision Point:** Accept or decline management

### Phase 2: Agreement & Onboarding

#### Step 3: Agreement Preparation
- Send property management agreement draft
- Outline terms, conditions, and service costs
- Specify 15% annual rent fee + VAT
- Detail all included services

#### Step 4: Contract Signing
- Both parties sign the agreement
- Client pays initial management fee
- Payment method: Invoice sent with bank details
- **Key Point:** Payment required at signing

#### Step 5: Key Handover
- Collect property keys from client
- **Options:** 
  - Client delivers to office
  - Team member collects from property

### Phase 3: Management Activation

#### Step 6A: For Already Rented Properties
- Immediate management takeover
- Review existing tenant agreements
- Establish communication with current tenants
- Begin rent collection and management duties

#### Step 6B: For Empty Properties
- Initiate rental process (see "Rent - New Listing" workflow)
- Find suitable tenants
- Execute rental agreements
- Transition to active management

## Included Management Services

### Property Maintenance
- Maintain apartment suitability for intended use
- Preserve furniture and equipment condition
- Handle repairs and maintenance issues
- Coordinate with building management

### Tenant Management
- Tenant screening and selection
- Lease agreement management
- Rent collection and financial reporting
- Handle tenant complaints and issues
- Ensure compliance with building regulations

### Financial Management
- Monthly rent collection
- Expense management and approval
- Financial reporting to owner
- Handle security deposits

### Building Relations
- Attend building community meetings
- Vote on behalf of owner with prior consent
- Manage neighbor relations
- Handle building-level issues

### Legal & Administrative
- Compliance with local regulations
- Tax documentation support
- Insurance coordination
- Legal issue management

## Termination Conditions

### Automatic Termination
- Property sale (agreement terminates automatically)

### Early Termination
- 30-day written notice required
- Email notice to: info@nest-land.com
- Return of advance payments for cancelled reservations

### Grounds for Immediate Termination
- Violation of agreement terms
- Non-fulfillment of administrator obligations
- Property destruction or obstruction of use (force majeure)

- Modifications require written owner approval
- Relationship: Independent contractors, not employment
